Event Scissor

The idea behind this application is collaboration. More specifically a collaboration from everyday people to small or midsize local business looking to make some extra money or promote their business. Event Scissor Property/Business owners rent out space for events. A user seeking to host an event can log in and enter the area code for the desired location of the event. From there they can see information on the space, the owner, and when they find the right space, they can correspond with the owner if they have any questions. Easy to find, book, and pay for an event space without the hassle of scouring the web for locations. At the same time you're giving owners a fun source of income and for businesses a free spotlight on their brand.
